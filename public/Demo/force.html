Health care seeking behaviour as a link between tuberculosis and socioeconomic factors

Study aim
Exploring the roles of socioeconomic status(SES) in care-seeking of Tuberculosis(TB) patients and how it affects the epidemiological features in Taiwan.

Field of interest
We are studying TB dynamics with linkages of socioeconomic status variables.

  • Deteminant: upper stream factors
  • Process: underlying behavours, can be conceptual or concrete
  • Transition: dynamic features, driven by processes
  • State: static features, changed through transitions
  • Outcome measurements for evaluation

    → Flow between states via transition.
